Accessibility Statement

Boscop is committed to making its websites, intranets, and extranets accessible in accordance with Article 47 of Law No. 2005-102 of February 11, 2005.

This accessibility declaration applies to orejime.boscop.fr.

Conformity status

orejime.boscop.fr is fully compliant with the RGAA General Accessibility Improvement Framework version 4.1.2.

Test results

The conformity audit carried out by Boscop reveals that 100% of RGAA criteria are respected.

Feedback and contact

If you cannot access a content or service, you can contact the person in charge of the website to be guided towards an accessible alternative or obtain the content in another format: contact Boscop

Recourse mechanisms

This procedure is to be used in the following case.
You have reported an accessibility defect to the website manager that prevents you from accessing a content or service of the portal and you have not received a satisfactory response.

  • Write a message to the Defender of Rights
  • Contact the Defender of Rights' delegate near you
  • Send a letter by mail (free, no stamp needed) to the Defender of Rights: Libre réponse, 71120 75342 Paris CEDEX 07
