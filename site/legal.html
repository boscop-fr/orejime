Privacy policy

While you browse this website, we (BOSCOP) may collect information about you: personal data! We are committed to ensuring that the collection and use of this data comply with regulations designed to protect personal data, including the well-known GDPR, the ePrivacy Directive (mainly concerning cookies), the French Data Protection Act (Cocorico), as well as various recommendations and guidelines from the CNIL and the EDPB.

Why do we collect your data?

Action logging (logs)

First and foremost, like on any website, technical traces of your visit are recorded when you load a page or resources (commonly known as the TCP-IP protocol ‚òùÔ∏è). Simply put, in order to display an image in your browser, our host's servers need to retrieve your IP address to send you the requested resource. In doing so, what we call "logs" are recorded on our host's servers, including the timestamp of the action and your IP address.

Beyond their use within the TCP-IP protocol, these logs help secure our website: ideally to prevent security incidents, but also to identify their origin if an incident occurs.

This data collection serves a legitimate interest of Boscop and our host: ensuring the proper and secure operation of its website. This interest remains within the reasonable expectations of a user, as action logging is a common practice.

Cookies

A "cookie" is a small file made up of letters and numbers, placed or read on your computer, for example, during your navigation on our website.

This website showcases a cookie manager, meaning that certain cookies may be placed on your device if you interact with the presented examples.

In practice, we only use anonymized audience measurement cookies (which do not involve the collection of personal data in the strict sense). These cookies allow us to understand how our website is used, assess its performance, generate statistics, and analyse traffic volumes and user interactions (visited sections, content engagement, navigation paths). This helps us improve the relevance and usability of our services.

To conduct these analyses, we use Matomo, an open-source audience measurement tool hosted on our own servers. The following cookies are used: "_pk_ref", "_pk_id", "_pk_ses", "visitor", "visit".

We have configured Matomo according to the CNIL-approved guidelines, meaning your consent is not required for their use. However, you always have the right to opt out!
